Powered by Acoustic Physics

OODIO's core engine uses the Schroeder Integration Method. Instead of trying to read noisy, raw reflections, we mathematically convert the impulse response into a perfectly smooth energy decay curve by integrating the squared audio samples backward in time.

Once your decay time is established, the Auto-BOM algorithm utilizes the Sabine Equation to prescribe the exact square footage of acoustic panels, diffusers, and bass traps required to hit your target metrics based on your room's cubic volume.
